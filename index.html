Work Experience

With over 9 years of experience, I have worked at various companies within manufacturing, oil and energy, finance and IT industry involving network design, maintenance, support, and systems administration in Windows Server environment and Active Directory infrastructure. Below is an overview of my work experiences:

Technical Engineer

June 2008 to May 2012

I started my career as Technical Engineer of a local IT service company. I was part of the Service department who is responsible for providing phone, e-mail and on-site technical support to external clients. Working in this company, we were trained to become flexible and fast learner. We were required to attend trainings, product launch, and bootcamps to familiarize ourselves to all the technologies that we support. It was overwhelming but incredibly fun. I was assigned as the SME for Storage and Backup support.

IT Consultant

August 2008 to February 2012

While working as a Technical Engineer, my manager sent me to one of our clients as an in-house IT consultant. The company is a global manufacturing company where I managed the company's local network and Active Directory infrastructure. During my stay, I handled several projects where I worked closely with my counterparts abroad and negotiate with vendors and external groups. From time to time, I trained users in IT security and other IT related stuffs.

Systems Administrator

May 2012 to January 2014

After almost 4 years in my previous company, I decided to quit to find a new job to further develop my IT skills. I was hired as a Systems Administrator for a multinational energy company. My stay here was my most engaging and challenging so far. Aside from my daily work routine as a Systems Administrator, I was given the chance to worked on several high-level projects like Active Directory, Exchange migration, file server design, disk and file encryption, deployment of client management system, and designing the IT service desk portal.

Security Administrator

January 2014 to present

As I progress in my career, I have decided to specialized in Security, something that I really wanted to focus on ever since. My current role as a Security Administrator involves user access management, participating in ongoing security reviews and audits, reviewing and improving Security Administration processes, process writing and documentation, and advising and educating the business on security standards and policies.

A little more about me

Personal Bio

Here’s a brief history of who I am: I grew up in Alaminos, Pangasinan, north of Manila, where I was raised by my grandparents. I lived in a house surrounded by fruit trees and rice fields. It was a very laid back and simple place. I lived there until I graduated in grade school. I moved to Caloocan City with my parents to continue my high school studies. During my second year, my parents bought us a personal computer. That's how I first got interested in computers so I decided to take computer studies. I enrolled at Colegio de San Juan de Letran where I took up BS in Information Technology with specialization in Systems Engineering. After graduation, I landed a job at a local IT service company. Since then, I have been employed as an IT professional.

When I am not glued to a computer screen, I spend my time reading books, watching films, and listening to music and audio books. Whenever I am not busy, I play badminton and bowling or go to the gym with friends and colleagues.
