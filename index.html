Work Experience

With over 12 years of experience, I have worked at various companies within manufacturing, oil and energy, finance and IT industry involving network and system design, implementation, and administration in Windows Server environment and Active Directory infrastructure. Below is an overview of my work experiences:

Technical Engineer

June 2008 to May 2012

I started my career as Technical Engineer for a local IT service company. I was part of the Service department responsible for providing phone, e-mail and on-site technical support to external clients. I was exposed to different IT products and solutions for SMEs and large enterprise. I was given the opportunity to improve my skills and be familiarized to different technologies by attending trainings, product launches, and bootcamps. It was overwhelming but incredibly fun. I was assigned as the SME for Storage and Backup support.

IT Consultant

August 2008 to February 2012

While working as a Technical Engineer, my manager assigned me as an in-house IT consultant to one of our clients. The company is a global manufacturing company where I managed the company's local network and Active Directory infrastructure. I handled several projects where I worked closely with my counterparts abroad, vendors and external groups. From time to time, I also trained users in IT security and other IT-related trends.

Systems Administrator

May 2012 to January 2014

After almost 4 years in my previous company, I looked for a new opportunity to further develop my IT skills. I was hired as a System Administrator for a multinational oil and energy company. It was my most engaging and challenging so far. Apart from my daily task administering the IT infrastructure, I was given the chance to worked on several high-level projects like Active Directory, Exchange migration, file server design, disk and file encryption, deployment of client management system, and designing the IT service desk portal.

IT Security - IAM

January 2014 to present

As I progress in my career, I decided to specialized in Security, something that I really wanted to focus on eversince. Luckily, I was given the opportunity to work for one of the largest investment companies under the Identity and Cybersecurity department. I'm part of the global team responsible for access control and identity lifecycle. These involve user access management, privilege access management, participating in ongoing security reviews and audits, reviewing and improving UAM processes, process writing and documentation, and advising and educating the business on security standards and policies.
